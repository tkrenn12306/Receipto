Questions

Things you're probably wondering.

Does Receipto work without internet?

Yes. The OCR runs entirely on your device using Apple's Vision framework. Capture, search and viewing all work offline. Cloud sync between your devices and backup happen when you're online.

What happens to my receipts? Where are they stored?

Receipt photos and data are stored in your private Firebase account on servers in Frankfurt, Germany — fully GDPR-compliant. You can export everything or delete your account at any time. We don't sell, share or analyse your data for advertising.

Which retailers does Receipto recognize?

200+ retailers across Germany, Austria and Switzerland are recognized automatically — including Saturn, MediaMarkt, IKEA, dm, Billa, Spar, Hofer, Migros, Coop, Apple, Hervis and more. For unknown retailers, you enter the merchant name once and we remember it.

Will it work for tax filing as a freelancer?

Yes — Pro lets you tag receipts as tax-relevant, then export everything as a structured PDF + CSV for your accountant at year-end. We support DATEV format too. Many freelancers save 2-3 hours of accountant fees this way.

What if I switch from Android or already use another app?

Receipto is iOS-only for now. If you're switching from another receipt app and have a CSV export, send it to us — we'll help you migrate.

Can I share receipts with my family?

Family Sharing arrives in V1.1 (mid-2026). Up to 6 family members will share one Pro subscription and shared receipts for big purchases.

How does the free trial work?

7 days free, full Pro access. Cancel anytime in your iPhone Settings before the trial ends and you won't be charged. We don't send you nag emails.
